Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 24, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us:

  • Account information: Email address, username, password (encrypted)
  • Preferences: Watched wallet addresses, alert settings, notification preferences
  • Payment information: Processed through Paddle (we do not store payment details)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Whale Wink service
  • Send you transaction alerts and notifications
  • Process subscription payments
  • Communicate with you about updates and support
  • Improve and optimize our service

3. Data We Monitor (Public Blockchain Data)

Whale Wink monitors public blockchain transactions. The wallet addresses you choose to watch are stored in your account. This data is publicly visible on the blockchain anyway — we simply aggregate and notify you about it.

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties. We may share data with:

  • Service providers: Paddle (payment processing), Resend (email delivery), Telegram (notifications)
  • Legal requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. Passwords are hashed using bcrypt. However, no method of electronic transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Cookies

We use essential cookies for session management and authentication. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Export your data

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@whalewink.net

8. Data Retention

We retain your account data as long as your account is active. You may delete your account at any time, which removes all personal data within 30 days.

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under 18 years of age.

10. Changes to Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify users of material changes via email.

11. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy should be sent to: support@whalewink.net
