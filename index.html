_FAQ

What is a whale in crypto?
A whale is a wallet that holds or moves large amounts of cryptocurrency. These can be exchanges, institutional investors, early adopters, or market makers. Their trades often influence price movements.
How do whale alerts work?
We monitor blockchain transactions in real-time across 9 networks. When a tracked wallet moves more than your set threshold, you get an instant Telegram (and email for Pro) alert with details.
Is this legal?
Yes! Whale tracking is completely legal. Public blockchain data is available to everyone. We're just making it easier to monitor and act on.
Can I track any wallet?
Yes, any public wallet address can be tracked. We provide lists of known whale addresses, or you can add your own.
What's the accuracy?
Our alerts are real-time from blockchain data. There's no delay. You get the alert as soon as the transaction confirms.
